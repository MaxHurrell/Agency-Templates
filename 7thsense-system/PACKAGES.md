# 7thsense — Service Packages

## How to Use
- Reference this when discussing pricing — never send as a raw file
- On calls: understand what they need first, then recommend ONE package
- Negotiate scope, not price — hold firm on pricing, adjust deliverables

---

## Starter — R7,500 (once-off)

**For:** Businesses with no website or an outdated one that need to get online properly.

### Deliverables
- 1 premium single-page website (all sections on one page, mobile-first)
- Up to 8 sections: Hero, About, Services (up to 6), Reviews, Team, FAQ, Contact, Footer
- Brand colours extracted from existing site or created for niche
- All real business data: address, hours, phone, services, Google reviews
- Click-to-call button on mobile
- WhatsApp button (if mobile number available)
- Tally.so contact form — enquiries emailed directly to client after go-live
- Google Maps embed
- Basic SEO: meta title, description, Open Graph tags, local business schema
- Hosting on Vercel (free, fast, reliable — no ongoing hosting cost)
- 1 round of copy/colour/image revisions

### Not Included
- Custom domain purchase (client buys — typically R150-200/year via Domains.co.za — Max connects it)
- Ongoing updates after launch
- Multi-page site
- Analytics dashboard
- Google Ads
- Email hosting

### Turnaround
- Demo ready: 24-48 hours from confirmed details
- Live on domain: 3-5 business days from approval and full payment

## Monthly Retainer — R2,500/month

**For:** Clients on any package who want their site actively managed.

**Minimum:** 3 months. Cancel anytime after with 30 days notice.

### Included Every Month
- Up to 4 content changes (new service, updated hours, staff change, image swap)
- Monthly Lighthouse performance check — fix any regressions
- Uptime monitoring — if site goes down, fixed within 24 hours
- Google review monitoring — alert on negative reviews
- Monthly PDF report: visitor count, form submissions, call clicks, top pages
- Priority WhatsApp support — response within 4 business hours
- Quarterly design refresh: new hero image, seasonal messaging, or visual update
- SSL and hosting maintained, DNS health checks

### Not Included
- Full redesigns (quoted separately)
- Google Ads management (R3,500/mo minimum, quoted separately)
- New pages beyond original scope (R1,500/page)
- Blog content writing (quoted per piece)
- Domain renewal (annual — handled by client or invoiced at cost)

## Objection Responses

**"That's expensive for a website"**
> "There are cheaper options — but those are templates where you do everything yourself. This is custom-built with your real data, professional images, and designed to get your phone ringing. And it's live in days, not weeks."

**"My nephew can build me a website"**
> "He probably can — go for it if that works. The difference here is this is built specifically to convert visitors into calls. It's not just looking good — it's being found on Google and making it dead easy for someone to contact you."

**"Can you do it for less?"**
> "I can adjust the scope — fewer sections, no automation. But I don't discount the work because the quality stays the same. What's most important to you?"

**"I need to think about it"**
> "Of course. The demo's live and not going anywhere. Message me whenever you're ready."

**"Can I pay monthly?"**
> "The build is once-off, but I can split it — half upfront, half on launch. After that, the monthly retainer is optional."
