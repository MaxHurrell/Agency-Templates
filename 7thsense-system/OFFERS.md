## OFFER A — The New Patient System (Risk Reversal)
*Best for: cold outreach, first client, proving the model*

**The offer:** I build your complete patient acquisition site first — your real data, reviews, services, everything. You look at it. If you love it and want it live on your domain, you pay R7,500. If you don't — delete my number.

**Risk removed:** The buyer pays nothing until they've seen the finished product and decided they want it. Their only risk is 5 minutes of time looking at a URL.

**Name:** The New Patient System (dental) / The Consultation Engine (law) / The Booked-Out Practice Programme (aesthetics)

**One-sentence pitch:** "I built your practice a site that's designed to make your phone ring more — have a look and tell me what you think."

**Buyer gets:** Live demo with their real data, reviews, brand. If they pay: live site on their domain within 5 days, Tally form wired to their email, click-to-call, WhatsApp, Google Reviews.

**We get:** R7,500 if they say yes. Portfolio piece either way. If 1 in 4 demos converts, effective rate is R1,875/hour of build time.

**When paid:** 50% on approval, 50% before go-live.

**Scorecard:**
- Buyer risk: ZERO (they only pay if they love it)
- Outcome specific: YES ("designed to make your phone ring more")
- ROI obvious: YES ("two new patients and it's paid for itself")
- Could say no and feel smart: HARD — they've already seen the product
- Named around outcome: YES

## OFFER B — The 90-Day Growth Guarantee (Performance)
*Best for: warm prospects who like the demo but hesitate*

**The offer:** I build and launch your complete patient acquisition site. If you don't receive at least 5 new enquiries through the site in the first 90 days, I continue optimising and managing it for free until you do.

**Guarantee wording:** "If your new site doesn't generate at least 5 new enquiries in 90 days — verifiable through the contact form submissions — I work for free until it does. No time limit. No excuses."

**Name:** The 90-Day Growth Guarantee

**One-sentence pitch:** "Five new enquiries in 90 days or I work free until you get them."

**Buyer gets:** Everything in Offer A plus: 90-day performance guarantee, monthly check-in, form submission tracking, one round of conversion optimisation if targets aren't hit.

**We get:** R12,500 (Growth package price justified by the guarantee). Monthly retainer conversation at 90 days when results are proven.

**Scorecard:**
- Buyer risk: NEAR ZERO (guaranteed outcome or free work)
- Outcome specific: YES (5 enquiries, 90 days, verifiable)
- ROI obvious: YES (5 enquiries × LTV = 10x+ return)
- Could say no and feel smart: VERY HARD
- Named around outcome: YES

## OFFER C — The Patient Pipeline (Revenue Share)
*Best for: law firms, high-end aesthetics, any business where one client = R10,000+*

**The offer:** I build and run your entire client acquisition system — site, conversion optimisation, form automation, review display. You pay nothing upfront. I receive R[X] for every new client above your current monthly baseline, verified by form submissions.

**Calculation:**
- Baseline: client's current monthly new enquiries (measured for 30 days before launch)
- After launch: track new form submissions monthly
- Delta: new submissions minus baseline = billable clients
- Fee: R500 per new dental patient / $500 per new legal consultation / $300 per new aesthetics booking
- Cap: 24 months, then the system is theirs free

**Example (dental, current 15 patients/month, grows to 25):**
- Delta: 10 new patients/month
- Our fee: 10 × R500 = R5,000/month
- Client's additional revenue: 10 × R6,700 LTV = R67,000 in lifetime value
- Client ROI: 1,340% (pays R5K, gains R67K in patient value)

**Name:** The Patient Pipeline (dental) / The Case Engine (law) / The Booking Machine (aesthetics)

**When paid:** Monthly, based on verified form submissions above baseline.

**Applies to:** Law (case value R50K+), high-end aesthetics (client LTV R15K+), established dental practices (20+ patients/month baseline). Does NOT apply to startups or businesses without existing patient flow.

**Scorecard:**
- Buyer risk: ZERO (pays nothing unless they're making more money)
- Outcome specific: YES (measurable delta above baseline)
- ROI obvious: YES (they keep 90%+ of the value created)
- Could say no and feel smart: IMPOSSIBLE — the math is undeniable
- Named around outcome: YES

## Offer Progression (natural sales sequence)

1. **Offer A** (cold outreach) → gets attention, proves capability
2. If interested but hesitant → **Offer B** (performance guarantee)
3. If high-value client after trust established → **Offer C** (revenue share)
4. Every client gets offered **retainer** (R2,500/month) at 90 days

Never skip steps. Earn the right to propose aligned incentives.
