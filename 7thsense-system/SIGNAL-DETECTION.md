## Cape Town Dental Market

### Signal 1: Good reviews, NO website (HIGH — Score 4/5)
**Evidence:** Google Maps listing with 4.0+ stars, 15+ reviews, website field empty or links to Facebook.
**Detection:** Google Maps → "dentist fish hoek" / "dentist muizenberg" / "dentist cape town southern suburbs" → check each listing's website field.
**Proves:** Patients trust them (reviews prove demand), but every Google searcher who wants to check them out online hits a dead end.
**Monthly cost:** (reviews × 50 impressions) × 5% CTR × 15% conversion gap × R6,700 LTV = R[X]
**Example (30 reviews):** 1,500 × 5% × 15% × R6,700 = ~R75,000/month in missed lifetime value
**Opening line:** "Dr [Name], you have [X] five-star reviews on Google — your patients clearly love you. But when someone Googles you and there's no website, they book somewhere else. I built what your site should look like: [URL]"

### Signal 2: Running Google Ads with weak site (CRITICAL — Score 5/5)
**Evidence:** Business appears in "Sponsored" results for "dentist cape town" but their landing page is slow, ugly, or has no booking form.
**Detection:** Search "dentist cape town" in incognito → note Sponsored results → click through → assess quality.
**Proves:** They're already paying per click (R15-R50/click in dental). If the landing page doesn't convert, every click is wasted.
**Monthly cost:** Estimated R5,000-R15,000/month ad spend × 67% waste rate (bad vs good conversion) = R3,350-R10,050/month burned.
**Opening line:** "I noticed you're running Google Ads for [keyword]. I checked your landing page — I think a significant portion of your ad spend is being wasted because visitors land on a page that doesn't make it easy to book. I built an alternative: [URL]"

### Signal 3: Active on social media but no site
**Evidence:** Business posts regularly on Instagram/Facebook but links to Facebook page, not a website.
**Detection:** Search "[practice name]" → if Instagram is the top result, not a .co.za → no website.
**Opening line:** "Your Instagram following is great — but people searching Google for a dentist in [area] can't find you. Here's what they should see: [URL]"

## Miami Aesthetics Market

### Signal 1: 5-star reviews but outdated website (HIGH)
**Evidence:** 4.5+ stars, 100+ reviews, but website looks like it was built in 2018. No mobile optimisation. No online booking.
**Detection:** Google Maps → "medspa miami beach" → click website → Firecrawl scrape → check mobile, forms, design age.
**Proves:** Patients love the experience but the website doesn't reflect it. The gap between review quality and site quality is the problem.
**Monthly cost:** 1,000 visitors × 2.5% conversion gap × $333/month client value = $8,325/month
**Opening line:** "Your patients rave about you — [rating] stars from [count] reviews. But your website doesn't show new visitors the experience your reviews describe. I built what it should look like: [URL]"

### Signal 2: Running Facebook/Instagram ads to a weak site (CRITICAL)
**Evidence:** Active ads visible in Facebook Ad Library sending traffic to a site with no booking form.
**Detection:** facebook.com/ads/library → search business name → if active ads exist → click through to landing page.
**Proves:** Paying for traffic to a page that doesn't convert. Every ad dollar is partially wasted.
**Opening line:** "I see you're running Instagram ads — smart move. But the page they land on doesn't have a booking button above the fold. You're paying to attract people and then losing them."

## Miami Law Market

### Signal 1: Running Google Ads for competitive keywords (CRITICAL)
**Evidence:** Firm appears in Sponsored results for "personal injury lawyer miami" (CPC $50-$200).
**Detection:** Search "car accident lawyer miami" / "immigration attorney miami" → note Sponsored results.
**Proves:** They're paying $50-$200 PER CLICK. At legal CPCs, a bad landing page is catastrophically expensive.
**Monthly cost:** Estimated 200 clicks/month × $100 CPC × 67% waste = $13,400/month wasted.
**Opening line:** "You're bidding on [keyword] — those clicks cost $100+. I checked your landing page. I think you're losing 2 out of every 3 visitors. I built a page designed to convert them: [URL]"

### Signal 2: Strong Avvo/Super Lawyer profile but weak website
**Evidence:** Attorney has 10.0 Avvo rating, Super Lawyer designation, but firm website is WordPress template from 2015.
**Detection:** Search "[attorney name] avvo" → check rating → then check firm website quality.
**Proves:** Their credentials are excellent but their website doesn't communicate it in the 3 seconds it matters.
**Opening line:** "You have a 10.0 on Avvo and Super Lawyer since [year]. But your website doesn't communicate that authority to someone in crisis who's comparing 3 firms in 30 seconds. I built what it should look like: [URL]"
