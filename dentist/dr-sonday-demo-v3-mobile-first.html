About Dr. Sonday Dentistry

Our Story

A Practice Built on Trust

Dr. S. Sonday, BChD, founded his practice with a clear vision: to provide the highest standard of dental care in a warm, welcoming environment where every patient is treated like family.

Over 20 years later, that vision remains at the heart of everything we do. From our treatment rooms to the friendly smiles that greet you at reception, every detail of our practice is designed with your comfort and care in mind.

As an HPCSA-registered practitioner, Dr. Sonday combines extensive clinical expertise with a genuinely compassionate approach. He takes time to listen to your concerns, explain your options clearly, and ensure you are comfortable with every step of your treatment plan.

Our team of dedicated professionals shares this commitment. Together, we have earned a perfect 5.0 Google rating and the trust of over 4,800 patients across Cape Town.

Our Philosophy

The Dr. Sonday Way

Three principles guide every decision we make, every treatment we recommend, and every interaction we have with our patients.

Patient-First Care

Your needs, concerns, and comfort always come first. We never rush a consultation or push unnecessary treatments. Your trust is our greatest asset, and we work hard to keep it.

Radical Honesty

We tell you what you need to hear, not what you want to hear. From diagnosis to pricing, every conversation is rooted in transparency. No upselling, no hidden costs, just honest care.

Quality Without Compromise

We use premium materials, proven techniques, and take the time to do things right. Your dental health is a long-term investment, and we treat it that way with every procedure.
