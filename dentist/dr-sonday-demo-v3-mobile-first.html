About Dr. Sonday

Dentistry with a Human Touch

Dr. S. Sonday has been caring for Ocean View and the surrounding Cape Town community for over two decades. His philosophy is simple: affordable, high-quality dentistry delivered with warmth and respect.

From your very first visit, you'll notice the difference — no rushing, no unnecessary procedures. Just honest care from a doctor who listens.

  • Qualified General Dentist (BChD)
  • Transparent pricing — no surprises
  • Gentle approach for anxious patients
