# 7thsense Opportunity Scanner (Josh-Level)

## Purpose
Find businesses that are already losing money because of a specific, detectable problem — then quantify that loss in rands or dollars before making contact.

This is not "find businesses that might want a website." This is "find businesses that are bleeding revenue right now and don't know it."

## The Signal-First Approach

Josh doesn't sell to anyone who might need his service. He finds people who are PROVABLY spending money inefficiently — then shows them the math. We do the same.

**Our signal:** Strong Google presence (reviews, Maps listing, ads) + weak or missing website conversion layer = money leaking every day.

## Signal Hierarchy

### CRITICAL — Score 5/5: Running Ads + Weak/No Website
**Detection:** Search "[niche] [city]" on Google. "Sponsored" results are running ads. Click through. If landing page is weak — CRITICAL.
**Also check:** Google Ads Transparency Center (adstransparency.google.com) — search business name.
**Also check:** Facebook Ad Library (facebook.com/ads/library) — search business name.
**Why critical:** They are ALREADY PAYING to send traffic somewhere. If that somewhere doesn't convert, every click is wasted money. This is Josh's exact pattern.

### HIGH — Score 4/5: Strong Reviews + No Website
**Detection:** Google Maps search → check each listing for website field. Empty or Facebook link = no website.
**Why high:** Word of mouth is working. Google is sending them impressions. But there's no conversion layer. Easiest close — no ego about existing site.

### HIGH — Score 4/5: Maps Ads Running + Weak Website
**Detection:** Mobile search "[niche] near me" — first 3 with green "Sponsored" checkmark.
**Why high:** Paying R20-R100 per lead through Maps. Website is the next thing the visitor sees.

### MEDIUM — Score 3/5: Good Reviews + Decent Site + No Lead Capture
**Detection:** Firecrawl scrape → check for form elements, tel: links, WhatsApp links, chat widgets.
**Why medium:** Site exists but has no conversion mechanism. Improvement opportunity, not urgent pain.

### LOW — Score 2/5: Good Reviews + Good Site + Basic Capture
**Detection:** Full audit. Minor optimisation opportunities.
**Not worth a cold demo.** Only approach if referred.

## Quantification Formulas

### Dental (South Africa — ZAR)
```
Monthly Maps impressions estimate: review_count × 50
Click-through rate: 5%
Current conversion (bad site): 5%
Good site conversion: 20%
Gap: 15%
Patient LTV: R6,700

FORMULA: (reviews × 50) × 5% × 15% × R6,700 = monthly opportunity cost
EXAMPLE: 30 reviews → R73,700/month in missed LTV
SIMPLIFIED: "You're losing 5-10 patients/month. That's R33K-R67K in lifetime value."
```

### Law (US — USD)
```
Estimated monthly traffic: 100-500 (varies by practice area and city)
Current conversion (bad site): 1%
Good site conversion: 4%
Gap: 3%
Average case value: $15,000-$50,000

FORMULA: traffic × 3% × case_value = monthly opportunity cost
EXAMPLE: 200 visitors × 3% × $20,000 = $120,000/month
SIMPLIFIED: "You're leaving 3-5 cases on the table every month."
```

### Aesthetics (US — USD)
```
Estimated monthly visitors: 500-3,000
Current conversion: 1.5%
Good site conversion: 4%
Gap: 2.5%
Client annual value: $4,000

FORMULA: visitors × 2.5% × ($4,000/12) = monthly opportunity cost
EXAMPLE: 1,000 visitors × 2.5% × $333 = $8,325/month
SIMPLIFIED: "Your reviews build trust. Your website drops the ball on converting it."
```

## Full Detection Workflow

1. **Define:** niche + city
2. **Maps scan:** search, extract top 20 listings rated 4.0+
3. **Website check:** for each — exists? quality? mobile? lead capture?
4. **Ads check:** Google search "[niche] [city]" — note sponsored results
5. **Facebook check:** Ad Library search for top 5 prospects
6. **Google Ads Transparency:** check each for active ad campaigns
7. **Score:** 1-5 based on signal hierarchy
8. **Quantify:** use niche formula for top 5
9. **Output:** ranked list with signal, score, monthly cost
10. **Action:** build demos for top 3 CRITICAL/HIGH before contact
