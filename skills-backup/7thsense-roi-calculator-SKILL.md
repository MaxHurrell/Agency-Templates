# 7thsense ROI Calculator (Josh-Level)

## Core Shift: Cost of the Problem, Not Value of the Solution

Josh doesn't say "my service is worth $X." He says "you're losing $X every month."

**Old approach:** "A new website will bring you more patients." (vague, unquantifiable)
**New approach:** "Based on your 30 Google reviews and no website, you're losing approximately R45,000 per month in patient lifetime value." (specific, painful, undeniable)

## The One-Number Pitch

Every outreach message must contain ONE number that makes the prospect stop scrolling:

- **Dental:** "You're likely losing R33,000-R67,000/month in patient lifetime value."
- **Law:** "Every week your site underperforms, that's $50,000-$100,000 in cases walking to a competitor."
- **Aesthetics:** "Your reviews build trust. Your website drops it. That gap is costing you $8,000+/month in bookings."

| Conversion rate (good site) | 15 – 25% | Industry benchmark |
| Conversion gap | 10 – 15% | Delta |

### Monthly Cost of Problem
```
FORMULA: (review_count × 50) × CTR × conversion_gap × LTV

CONSERVATIVE: (reviews × 50) × 5% × 10% × R6,700
BASE:         (reviews × 50) × 10% × 12% × R6,700
OPTIMISTIC:   (reviews × 50) × 15% × 15% × R6,700

EXAMPLE (30 reviews):
Conservative: 1,500 × 5% × 10% × R6,700 = R50,250/month in missed LTV
Base:         1,500 × 10% × 12% × R6,700 = R120,600/month
Optimistic:   1,500 × 15% × 15% × R6,700 = R226,125/month
```

| Package | Price | Patients needed to break even | Time to break even |
|---------|-------|-------------------------------|-------------------|

| Growth (R12,500) | R12,500 | 1.9 patients | 30-60 days |
| Full (R18,500) | R18,500 | 2.8 patients | 60-90 days |

### Pitch Lines

## The "Burning Money" Argument (for businesses running ads)

This is the most powerful argument in the system. Adapt for any niche:

```
FORMULA: monthly_ad_spend × (1 - (bad_conversion / good_conversion)) = monthly waste

EXAMPLE (dental running R10,000/month Google Ads):
Bad landing page conversion: 5%
Good landing page conversion: 15%
Wasted portion: 1 - (5/15) = 67%
Monthly waste: R10,000 × 67% = R6,700/month WASTED

PITCH: "You're spending R10,000/month on Google Ads. Based on your landing page,
roughly R6,700 of that is being wasted every month. Our site costs R7,500 once.
It pays for itself in the first month from ad savings alone."
```
